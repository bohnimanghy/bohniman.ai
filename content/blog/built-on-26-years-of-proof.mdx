There's a version of the AI story that starts in 2023. Ours starts in 1999.

For over two decades, Bohniman Systems built the systems governments can't
afford to have fail — digitizing **2 crore citizen records**, powering education
departments, and delivering World Bank projects. Downtime wasn't a line item.
It wasn't an option.

## Discipline is the moat

Anyone can prototype an AI feature. Shipping it into a workflow that thousands
of people depend on — every day, without drama — is a different discipline
entirely. That discipline has a name in our world:

- **CMMI Level 3** appraised processes
- **ISO 27001** certification — the first in Northeast India
- **250+ projects** delivered, every one successful

These aren't badges. They're the reason our AI products behave in production the
way they behave in the demo.

## AI where it actually matters

We don't build AI to impress. We build it where it removes real operational
pain — events, kitchens, clinics, classrooms, and 22 Indic languages.

> The best AI product is the one your team forgets is AI. It just works.

That's the standard. Twenty-six years got us here. The next twenty-six start
now.
